Put your skills to the test.

WHAT: You will be given a large series of problems of increasing difficulty. With a team of up to 4 people, you will compete to solve as many of these problems as you can in the time allotted.

WHO: Students in grades 8-12

WHEN: Sunday, October 14th, 12pm-5:30pm

WHERE: UW Madison CS Building, Room 310

WHAT TO BRING: Two computers per team (one for competition and one for backup), charger, parental waiver (if not turned in online), extra snacks.


Details

The Equinox Programming Challenge is a programming competition for highschoolers (and 8th graders) in the Madison area. Students, in teams of up to four, can use any language to complete as many problems as possible in the alotted four hours. The competition will be hosted on HackerRank which compiles many languages, including Java, Python and more!
